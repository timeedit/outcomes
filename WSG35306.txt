["WSG35306","Modelling Future Water Stress","2MO","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- critically discuss global water quantity issues such as water shortage for human uses (e.g., cities, agriculture) and flooding in relation to water quality issues related to nutrients, water temperature, pathogens, salinity, plastic and toxic compounds;- assess the skill and output of integrated modelling of water quantity and water quality issues;- use simple global water system models;- design and apply an indicator for water stress that takes into account both water quantity and water quality.",""]
