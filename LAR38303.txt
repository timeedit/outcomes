["LAR38303","Landscape Aesthetics","5MO","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- compare different conceptions of art and aesthetic value (beautiful, sublime and picturesque);- identify characteristics of contemporary landscape architecture and relate them to aesthetic theories;- deduce the ethical and aesthetical consequences of the position of design between science and art;- assess the discourse of contemporary environmental aesthetics;- distinguish different techniques to gather and represent data on the experience of landscapes;- compose an informed critique by means of a written paper.",""]
