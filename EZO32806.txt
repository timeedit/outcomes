["EZO32806","Biomimetics","4WD","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:use the language from different fields in order to communicate effectively about biomimetics in an interdisciplinary group environment;describe the subfields of biomimetics, ranging from robotics to medicine to industrial design;use reverse engineering to measure functional aspects of a biological system, such as adhesive performance;identify the biomimetic potential (and limitations) of biological systems;design biomimetic technical systems by the abstraction, transfer and application of knowledge gained from biological systems;plan and execute a mini-research project, including written reports and an oral presentation.",""]
