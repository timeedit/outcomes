["AEP30306","Economics of EU policies for sustainability transitions","5AF","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- understand and explain central economic concepts, political rationales and the evolution of EU green sustainability transition;- explain economic rationales of EU policies aimed at agriculture, food and regional development;- apply micro-economic and regional economic tools to assess impacts of agricultural, trade and regional policies on stakeholders at various spatial levels;- evaluate economic instruments implemented for specific policy aims;- compare and analyse selected aspects of scientific articles and policy documents;- design an independent socio-economic analysis;- compose a scientific research proposal;- appraise the research proposals of peer students by constructively communicating high-quality feedback.",""]
