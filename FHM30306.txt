["FHM30306","Food Safety Risk Assessment","5MO","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- have an overview of the occurrence and characteristics of food-borne pathogens;- debate the effect of processing on the occurrence of toxic compounds and the level of pathogens present;- distinguish host responses to pathogens and their toxins;- discuss the role of gut microbiota in toxicological risks of food-borne chemicals;- compare and apply dose response models;- build models to estimate microbiological and toxicological risks;- interpret and compare risk estimates;- formulate advice taking into account microbiological and toxicological risk estimates in a written report.",""]
