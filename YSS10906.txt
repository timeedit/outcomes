["YSS10906","Analysis of a Problem Situation","6AF","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- jointly recognise a complex societal problem through the precise phrasing of a problem statement;- jointly demonstrate -  through the drafting of a research proposal -  the relation between a complex societal problem and the applicability of scientific knowledge, as well as the ethical aspects entailed;- demonstrate, both jointly and individually, that they are proficient in obtaining scientific data according to a pre-established work plan;- prove, both jointly and individually, that they can systematically apply disciplinary and thematic knowledge and skills to the analysis of scientific data;- make evident, as a group, that they can provide an account of the analysis of scientific data by means of a report of 7,500 words (and presentation thereof) that aims to propose a solution to a concrete societal problem.",""]
