["EZO30806","Functional Zoology","5MO","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:use and apply the basic theoretical concepts and techniques used in current functional zoology;select and apply a range of experimental techniques with appropriate practical skills;execute a basic error analysis of measurements;make a mechanistic analysis of animal movement systems;program in Matlab, a state-of-the-art software environment for numerical problems;create analytical and numerical models of time-dependent movement systems in zoology;judge and test the validity of numerical models with experimental outcomes;design and execute mini research projects, including written and oral presentations;organise their own learning processes based on reflection upon the acquisition of new knowledge in the field of functional zoology.",""]
