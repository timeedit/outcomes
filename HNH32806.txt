["HNH32806","Exposure Assessment in Nutrition and Health Research","5MO","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- design and develop food frequency questionnaires;- design an evaluation study to compare the performance of a food frequency questionnaire against one or more reference methods; - explain the aims and principles of evaluation studies in the context of nutritional research;- understand how errors in dietary assessment affect the interpretation of results from nutrition surveillance and nutritional epidemiology and how evaluation studies can be used to obtain estimates of potential errors;- understand the analysis of evaluation studies on dietary assessment, including the use of biomarkers in nutritional epidemiologic research.",""]
