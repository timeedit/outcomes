["ENT20806","Integrated Pest Management","6AF","After successful completion of this course students are expected to be able to:- explain different approaches towards breeding for disease resistance;- explain the main factors that are important to understand the epidemiology of pests and diseases;- describe the different methods of pest management to be used for the prevention of damage caused by plant pests, diseases and weeds, with an emphasis on environmentally sound and safe methods of pest control;- develop decisions about integrated pest management by focusing on the interactions between plant pests and the abiotic and biotic factors that affect these pests and their population growth over time and distribution in the field.",""]
